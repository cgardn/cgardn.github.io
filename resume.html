Frontend Developer: 2020 - present

I've worked mostly in eCommerce, for my current employer Luxury Brand Partners. I develop and maintain several of our brand sites, and add smaller features to other smaller properties and marketing sites as well. My focus here has been on performance, as I inherited several sites that were heavy on third-party libraries, no code-splitting, and generally not optimized for mobile (which is 80%+ of our users). In my time, I've reduced bundle sizes by over 60% on multiple sites, and led internal efforts to implement a mobile-first content policy for images and videos. I've also developed several key features for our customers (bulk-ordering system on our Pro site), and multiple high-end interactive product launch experiences for various brands. Stack:

  • HTML, CSS, SVG, SASS
  • native JS, VueJS, NodeJS, jQuery (when forced :) )
  • Webpack, Rollup
  • Vue Storefront, Shopify
Before my current employer, I developed an unreleased social media app in VueJS with a small team in 2020.
